Hey! I'm Sai Tharun — a software engineer with a deep curiosity for how things work. My forte lies in building scalable, data-driven applications and modernizing systems for the cloud, with a growing focus on applied AI. I enjoy transforming complex systems into efficient, cloud-native solutions and leveraging data to drive smarter decisions and better user experiences.

I'm a firm believer in the idea that "Growth ceases when learning stops", which is why I continuously explore new technologies — from distributed data systems to modern frameworks and AI-driven solutions. Outside of code, you'll find me planning my next travel adventure, organizing events, or getting lost in a new course on something completely different. I'm an extrovert who thrives in collaborative environments and enjoys building alongside diverse, driven teams — so don't be shy!
